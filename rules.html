Server Rules

Play fair, be respectful, and have fun. Breaking these rules may result in a mute, kick, or ban.

General Conduct

  • 1Treat all players and staff with respect. No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination.
  • 2No griefing, stealing, or raiding land-claimed locations.
  • 3Keep chat appropriate. No excessive swearing, sexual content, or toxic behaviour.
  • 4No spamming chat, commands, or signs.
  • 5Do not impersonate staff or other players.

Gameplay

  • 6No hacking, cheating, or mods that give an unfair advantage (kill aura, x-ray, speed hacks).
  • 7No AFK machines or auto-clickers without staff approval.
  • 8Lag machines or crash-inducing builds are strictly prohibited.
  • 9Exploiting bugs must be reported to staff immediately — not abused.
  • 10PvP is allowed where permission has been given (e.g. PvP arena, wildnerness, land claims with PvP on)

Chat & Communication

  • 11English is the primary language in global chat.
  • 12No advertising other servers or websites without permission.
  • 13Do not share personal information about yourself or others.
  • 14Political and religious debates belong elsewhere, not in-game.

Economy & Trading

  • 15Do not deliberately crash the economy through price manipulation.
  • 16Real-money trading (RMT) for in-game items is strictly prohibited.
  • 17Chargebacks on store purchases result in a permanent ban.

Building

  • 18Inappropriate builds (sexual, hateful, or offensive structures) are prohibited.

Consequences

  • 191st offense: verbal warning.
  • 202nd offense: temporary mute or kick.
  • 213rd offense: temporary ban (duration based on severity).
  • 22Severe violations (hacking, doxing, threats) = immediate permanent ban.
  • 23You may appeal bans at appeal.html.

Rules last updated May 2025 · Staff have final say in all disputes
